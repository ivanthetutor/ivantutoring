Electronics

FFKM and rubber sealing for electronics and electrical applications.

Electronics Applications

Electronic devices require compact, reliable seals that safeguard against moisture, dust, and environmental exposure. Our elastomers and LSR (liquid silicone rubber) materials support miniaturization, aesthetic requirements, and rapid assembly processes.

From handheld devices to telecom infrastructure and industrial controls, we deliver sealing solutions that preserve performance, extend product life, and satisfy demanding ingress protection (IP) ratings.

Key Requirements:

  • Ingress protection against water, dust, and contaminants
  • Low compression set for thin-section seals
  • Dielectric strength and insulation performance
  • Compatibility with adhesives and housing materials
  • UV and weathering resistance for outdoor electronics
  • High-volume manufacturing consistency

Recommended Materials

Liquid Silicone Rubber (LSR)

Temperature Range: -50°C to +200°C

Key Benefits: Excellent flexibility, biocompatibility, and clarity

Applications: Keypads, wearable devices, consumer electronics gaskets

Conductive Elastomers

Temperature Range: -40°C to +160°C

Key Benefits: EMI/RFI shielding with reliable sealing

Applications: Enclosures, antenna systems, military electronics

EPDM & TPE

Temperature Range: -40°C to +135°C

Key Benefits: Excellent weathering resistance and flexibility

Applications: Outdoor housings, lighting fixtures, sensor covers

FKM

Temperature Range: -20°C to +200°C

Key Benefits: Chemical resistance to cleaning agents and fuels

Applications: Connector seals, instrumentation, industrial controls

Specific Applications

Consumer Devices

Button membranes, speaker gaskets, and waterproof seals for smartphones, tablets, and wearables requiring tactile precision and aesthetic quality.

Telecom & Networking

Outdoor enclosure seals, EMI shielding gaskets, and weatherproofing components for antennas, routers, and base stations.

Industrial Electronics

Seals and gaskets for control panels, sensors, and automation equipment exposed to oils, dust, and temperature extremes.

Medical & Life Sciences Electronics

Biocompatible seals and housings for diagnostic equipment, monitoring devices, and sterilizable instruments.
