Garlast Specialty Seals

Our Garlast specialty seal line encompasses advanced sealing geometries designed for superior performance in challenging applications. These specialized seal profiles offer enhanced sealing characteristics, reduced friction, and improved durability compared to standard O-rings.

From X-rings with dual sealing lips to U-rings and V-rings for rod and piston applications, our specialty seals provide reliable solutions for hydraulic systems, rotating equipment, and high-performance machinery.

Key Features:

  • Multiple sealing lips for enhanced performance
  • Reduced friction and improved wear resistance
  • Available in multiple elastomer compounds
  • Custom dimensions and configurations
  • High-volume production capabilities
  • Technical support and application engineering

Product Types

Garlast X-Rings

Design: Quad-ring with four sealing lips

Benefits: Lower friction, reduced rolling, dual sealing

Applications: Hydraulic cylinders, pneumatic systems, rotating shafts

Garlast U-Rings

Design: U-shaped cross-section with sealing lips

Benefits: Excellent sealing, easy installation

Applications: Rod seals, piston seals, hydraulic systems

Garlast V-Rings

Design: V-shaped profile for dynamic sealing

Benefits: Self-energizing, compensates for wear

Applications: Rotary shaft seals, bearing seals, rotating equipment

Garlast Gaskets

Types: Die-cut, molded, custom shapes

Materials: NBR, EPDM, FKM, Silicone, Garlast™ FFKM

Applications: Flange sealing, equipment covers, static applications

Garlast Hydraulic Seals

Types: Piston seals, rod seals, wipers, backup rings

Benefits: High pressure capability, low friction

Applications: Hydraulic cylinders, actuators, power units

Applications

Hydraulic Systems

High-performance seals for hydraulic cylinders, pumps, valves, and actuators in industrial and mobile equipment.

Pneumatic Equipment

Low-friction seals for pneumatic cylinders, actuators, and control systems requiring efficient operation.

Rotating Equipment

Specialty seals for rotating shafts, bearings, and machinery requiring dynamic sealing performance.

High-Performance Machinery

Advanced sealing solutions for demanding applications requiring superior wear resistance and sealing reliability.
