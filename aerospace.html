Aerospace & Defense

FFKM and rubber o-rings for extreme aerospace and defense environments.

Aerospace & Defense Applications

The aerospace and defense industry demands the highest levels of performance, reliability, and safety from sealing components. Our specialized rubber materials and sealing solutions are engineered to withstand extreme conditions including high altitudes, temperature fluctuations, pressure variations, and exposure to aggressive fluids.

From commercial aircraft to military defense systems, spacecraft to unmanned vehicles, our sealing solutions ensure mission-critical reliability in the most demanding environments where failure is not an option.

Key Requirements:

  • Extreme temperature resistance (-65°C to +200°C)
  • High pressure and vacuum compatibility
  • Excellent aging and weathering resistance
  • Low outgassing properties
  • Fire resistance and smoke generation control
  • Compliance with aerospace standards

Recommended Materials

Garlast® FFKM

Temperature Range: -20°C to +327°C

Key Benefits: Exceptional chemical resistance, ultra-low outgassing

Applications: Fuel systems, hydraulic systems, engine components

FKM (Fluoroelastomer)

Temperature Range: -20°C to +200°C

Key Benefits: Excellent fuel and oil resistance, good aging

Applications: Engine seals, fuel system components

EPDM

Temperature Range: -50°C to +150°C

Key Benefits: Excellent weathering, ozone resistance

Applications: Environmental seals, cabin systems

NBR

Temperature Range: -40°C to +120°C

Key Benefits: Good oil resistance, cost-effective

Applications: General purpose seals, hydraulic systems

Specific Applications

Commercial Aircraft

Engine seals, fuel system components, hydraulic systems, cabin pressurization, landing gear systems, and environmental control systems.

Military Defense Systems

Weapon systems, radar equipment, communication systems, navigation equipment, and mission-critical components requiring high reliability.

Spacecraft & Satellites

Propulsion systems, life support systems, thermal control systems, and components requiring ultra-low outgassing properties.

Unmanned Systems

Drone components, autonomous vehicle systems, and remote sensing equipment requiring lightweight, reliable sealing solutions.
