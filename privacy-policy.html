Privacy Policy

How Garlast collects and uses information on this website.

Last updated: May 18, 2026

Garlast Shanghai Sealing Technology Co. LTD (“Garlast,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) operates this informational website about rubber seals, O-rings, FFKM compounds, gaskets, and related technical references. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices available to you when you visit our site or submit our contact form.

This website is a static, informational site. We do not operate an online store, shopping cart, or payment checkout on this website.

Information We Collect

Information you provide

If you use the Contact Form, we collect the information you enter, which may include:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number and country code
  • Company name
  • Products of interest (checkbox selections)
  • Comments or questions (free-text only)

The contact form accepts text and selections only. It does not support file uploads, attachments, or payment information.

Information collected automatically

Like most websites, our hosting and form-processing services may automatically collect limited technical data when you visit the site, such as IP address, browser type, device type, referring URL, and general usage logs. We use this information to maintain site security and reliability, not for targeted advertising on this website.

How We Use Information

We use the information described above to:

  • Respond to quote requests, technical inquiries, and general questions
  • Route your message to the appropriate Garlast sales or engineering contact
  • Improve our website content and customer support
  • Protect against spam, abuse, and unauthorized access
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations

We do not sell your personal information.

Form Submission Service

Contact form submissions are transmitted through a third-party email delivery service (FormSubmit) so that your message can be delivered to our team. That provider processes the form data on our behalf in order to forward your inquiry. We recommend reviewing their privacy practices if you require additional detail about their handling of submission data.

Cookies and Similar Technologies

This website does not use advertising cookies or third-party marketing trackers. Your browser may store standard session or preference data locally. If we introduce optional analytics or functional cookies in the future, we will update this policy accordingly.

How Long We Keep Information

We retain contact form submissions and related correspondence for as long as needed to respond to your inquiry, manage an active quote or customer relationship, meet record-keeping requirements, and resolve disputes. When information is no longer required, we delete or anonymize it where practicable.

How We Share Information

We may share information only in these situations:

  • With service providers that help us operate the website or deliver form submissions
  • With Garlast affiliates or personnel involved in responding to your request
  • When required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of Garlast, our customers, or others

International Visitors

Garlast is based in China and serves customers internationally. If you submit information from outside your home country, it may be processed in China or other locations where Garlast or its service providers operate.

Your Choices and Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict certain processing of your personal information. To make a request, contact us using the details below. We may need to verify your identity before responding.

Children’s Privacy

This website is intended for business and professional use. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent revision. Continued use of the website after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or requests, contact Garlast at info@garlast.com or +1 (510) 392-4188.
