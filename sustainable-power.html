Sustainable Power

Rubber and FFKM sealing for renewable and sustainable power systems.

Sustainable Power Applications

Renewable energy systems operate in challenging outdoor environments, requiring seals that resist UV exposure, weathering, and thermal cycling. Energy storage and hydrogen systems demand materials compatible with new chemistries and high voltages.

Garlast provides elastomers and engineered components for wind, solar, hydrogen, and battery applications that deliver airtight sealing, electrical insulation, and long service life.

Key Requirements:

  • UV, ozone, and weathering resistance
  • High dielectric strength and insulation properties
  • Compatibility with coolants, electrolytes, and hydrogen
  • Low-temperature flexibility for harsh climates
  • Robust sealing under thermal cycling and vibration
  • Lightweight materials for efficiency and ease of installation

Recommended Materials

EPDM

Temperature Range: -50°C to +150°C

Key Benefits: Excellent weathering, ozone, and UV resistance

Applications: Solar panel gaskets, wind turbine nacelles, outdoor enclosures

Silicone

Temperature Range: -60°C to +200°C

Key Benefits: Superior thermal stability and dielectric properties

Applications: Battery pack seals, EV charging infrastructure, high-voltage insulation

FKM

Temperature Range: -20°C to +200°C

Key Benefits: Chemical resistance to coolants, lubricants, and fuels

Applications: Hydrogen systems, inverters, energy storage modules

Garlast® FFKM

Temperature Range: -20°C to +327°C

Key Benefits: Maximum chemical resistance for aggressive media

Applications: Electrolyzers, fuel cells, concentrated solar thermal systems

Specific Applications

Wind Energy

Blade root seals, nacelle gaskets, and pitch control seals engineered for continuous motion and harsh offshore/offshore conditions.

Solar Power

Weatherproof seals for module frames, junction boxes, and tracking systems that endure UV exposure and temperature extremes.

Energy Storage

Battery cell and pack sealing, thermal management gaskets, and dielectric barriers for lithium-ion and solid-state systems.

Hydrogen & Fuel Cells

Hydrogen-compatible seals, electrolyte containment, and compression gaskets meeting stringent permeability and safety requirements.
