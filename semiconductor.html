Semiconductors

High-purity FFKM perfluoroelastomer o-rings for semiconductor manufacturing.

Semiconductor Applications

Semiconductor fabrication requires materials with exceptional purity, plasma resistance, and low particle generation. Our sealing components are designed for wafer processing, lithography, etch, deposition, and metrology equipment where contamination control is paramount.

Garlast works closely with tool manufacturers and fabs to deliver repeatable performance, extended maintenance intervals, and reliable sealing in aggressive fluorinated chemistries and high-temperature vacuums.

Key Requirements:

  • Ultra-low metallic and ionic extractables
  • Minimal outgassing and particle shedding
  • Plasma, ozone, and reactive gas resistance
  • Dimensional stability in vacuum and high temperature
  • Compatibility with advanced cleans and CMP slurries
  • Traceability and clean-room packaging

Recommended Materials

Garlast® FFKM

Temperature Range: -20°C to +327°C

Key Benefits: Ultra-high purity, plasma resistance, low outgassing

Applications: Chamber seals, slit valves, gas delivery systems

Peroxide-Cured FKM

Temperature Range: -15°C to +220°C

Key Benefits: Excellent chemical resistance with reduced metal content

Applications: Vacuum door seals, wet process equipment

High-Purity EPDM

Temperature Range: -40°C to +150°C

Key Benefits: Superior steam and DI water resistance, low leachables

Applications: CMP systems, cleaning modules, chemical delivery

PTFE & PFA Components

Temperature Range: -200°C to +260°C

Key Benefits: Extreme chemical inertness and cleanliness

Applications: Valve seats, pump diaphragms, manifolds
