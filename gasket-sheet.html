Gasket & Sheet Materials

Our gasket and sheet material portfolio provides versatile sealing solutions for static applications across multiple industries. From food-grade gaskets for sanitary applications to high-voltage gaskets for electrical equipment, we offer materials engineered for specific performance requirements.

Available as sheet stock, die-cut gaskets, extruded sealing strips, and custom-machined components, our materials deliver reliable sealing performance in demanding environments.

Key Features:

  • Multiple material options and compounds
  • Sheet stock and custom-cut gaskets
  • Food-grade and FDA-compliant options
  • High-voltage and electrical grade materials
  • PTFE sheets, films, and machined parts
  • Custom dimensions and configurations

Product Types

Food-Grade Gaskets

Standards: FDA 21 CFR 177.2600, NSF 51

Materials: EPDM, Silicone, NBR, PTFE

Applications: Food processing, beverage, pharmaceutical equipment

High-Voltage Gaskets

Properties: Electrical insulation, tracking resistance

Materials: Silicone, EPDM, specialty compounds

Applications: Electrical enclosures, switchgear, transformers

Rubber Sealing Strip

Forms: Extruded profiles, custom shapes

Materials: EPDM, NBR, Silicone, Neoprene

Applications: Door seals, window seals, equipment enclosures

Teflon (PTFE) Sheets/films

Forms: Sheets, films, tapes, machined parts

Properties: Chemical inert, low friction, high temperature

Applications: Chemical processing, high-temperature sealing, non-stick surfaces

Garlast Gaskets

Types: Die-cut, molded, custom shapes

Materials: Full range of elastomers available

Applications: Flange sealing, equipment covers, general static sealing

Applications

Food & Beverage Processing

FDA-compliant gaskets and sealing materials for sanitary applications in food processing and beverage production equipment.

Electrical & Electronics

High-voltage gaskets and insulating materials for electrical enclosures, switchgear, and electronic equipment.

Chemical Processing

Chemical-resistant gaskets and PTFE materials for aggressive chemical environments and high-temperature applications.

General Industrial

Versatile gasket materials and sealing strips for equipment covers, flanges, and general-purpose static sealing applications.
