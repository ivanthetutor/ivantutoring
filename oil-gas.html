Oil & Gas Refining

FFKM and rubber o-rings for oil & gas refining and production.

Oil & Gas Applications

Oil and gas facilities demand elastomers that can tolerate high pressure, temperature extremes, sour gas exposure, and abrasive media. Our materials provide reliable sealing performance in upstream drilling, production, gas processing, and refining operations.

We design seals and gaskets that reduce maintenance, prevent leaks, and support safety-critical equipment in onshore, offshore, and subsea environments.

Key Requirements:

  • High pressure/high temperature (HPHT) capability
  • Resistance to sour gas (H2S) and aggressive hydrocarbons
  • Compression and extrusion resistance in dynamic sealing
  • Fire resistance and low compression set
  • Certification to API, NORSOK, ISO, and NACE standards
  • Longevity in abrasive, sand-laden media

Recommended Materials

Garlast™ FFKM

Temperature Range: -20°C to +327°C

Key Benefits: Exceptional sour gas and chemical resistance

Applications: Critical valve seals, compressor systems, high acid environments

HNBR

Temperature Range: -40°C to +150°C

Key Benefits: Excellent mechanical strength and resistance to drilling fluids

Applications: Blowout preventers, packers, downhole tools

FKM

Temperature Range: -20°C to +200°C

Key Benefits: High temperature performance and chemical resistance

Applications: Refinery pumps, flanges, pipeline components

EPDM & AFLAS™

Temperature Range: -25°C to +200°C

Key Benefits: Steam, amine, and sour gas resistance

Applications: Gas sweetening units, steam injection systems, chemical processing

Specific Applications

Upstream Exploration

Seals for drilling equipment, rotating heads, and completion tools designed for HPHT and sour gas conditions.

Midstream Transport

Pipeline gaskets, valve seals, and compressor components that prevent leaks and withstand fluctuating pressures.

Downstream Refining

High-temperature seals for pumps, reactors, and furnace components handling aggressive hydrocarbons and process chemicals.

Offshore & Subsea Systems

Elastomers engineered for saltwater corrosion, hydrostatic pressure, and long-term reliability in subsea production.
