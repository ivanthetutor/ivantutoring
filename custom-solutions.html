Custom Sealing Solutions

When standard products don't meet your requirements, our engineering team develops custom sealing solutions tailored to your specific application needs. We combine material science expertise, precision manufacturing capabilities, and application engineering to deliver solutions that optimize performance, reliability, and cost-effectiveness.

From custom material formulations to complex geometries, bonded assemblies, and overmolded components, we provide end-to-end support from concept through production.

Key Capabilities:

  • Custom material formulations and compounding
  • Complex geometry design and manufacturing
  • Bonded assemblies and overmolding
  • Prototype development and validation
  • Production tooling and manufacturing
  • Full documentation and quality certification

Custom Solution Types

Custom Material Formulations

Capabilities: Specialty compounds, custom properties

Process: R&D, testing, validation, production

Applications: Unique media, extreme conditions, regulatory requirements

Complex Geometries

Types: Non-standard shapes, large diameters, thin cross-sections

Manufacturing: Precision molding, machining, assembly

Applications: Unique designs, space constraints, performance optimization

Bonded Assemblies

Types: Metal-to-elastomer, plastic-to-elastomer, multi-material

Benefits: Integrated solutions, reduced assembly time

Applications: Complex components, OEM assemblies, integrated systems

Overmolded Components

Process: Injection molding, compression molding

Benefits: Precise fit, reduced assembly, enhanced performance

Applications: Sealed connectors, integrated seals, complex parts

Development Process

Requirements Analysis

Our engineering team works with you to understand application requirements, operating conditions, performance targets, and constraints to define the optimal solution approach.

Design & Development

We develop custom designs, material formulations, and manufacturing processes tailored to your specifications, with CAD modeling and FEA analysis as needed.

Prototype & Validation

Rapid prototyping and comprehensive testing ensure the solution meets all performance criteria before production tooling and manufacturing.

Production & Support

Full-scale production with quality assurance, documentation, and ongoing technical support to ensure consistent performance and reliability.
