Chemical Handling & Processing

FFKM and rubber seals for chemical handling and processing equipment.

Chemical Processing Applications

Chemical plants require sealing systems that maintain integrity in aggressive environments, prevent fugitive emissions, and protect personnel. Our elastomers and PTFE-based materials are developed to resist concentrated acids, alkalis, solvents, and high-temperature steam.

Garlast solutions deliver low-permeation seals, long service intervals, and proven compatibility with a vast chemical media library, reducing unplanned outages and maintenance costs.

Key Requirements:

  • Outstanding chemical and solvent resistance
  • Temperature tolerance from cryogenic to +327°C
  • Low permeability and emissions performance
  • Compression set resistance for flange sealing
  • Static and dynamic sealing capability
  • Compliance with environmental and safety regulations

Recommended Materials

Garlast™ FFKM

Temperature Range: -20°C to +327°C

Key Benefits: Ultimate chemical resistance and ultra-low permeation

Applications: Mechanical seals, pump shafts, analyzer systems

FKM (Fluoroelastomer)

Temperature Range: -20°C to +200°C

Key Benefits: Excellent resistance to hydrocarbons and solvents

Applications: Valve stem packing, reactor seals, compressor systems

EPDM

Temperature Range: -50°C to +150°C

Key Benefits: Superior steam, water, and polar solvent resistance

Applications: Steam service gaskets, CIP/SIP equipment, water treatment

PTFE & Filled PTFE

Temperature Range: -200°C to +260°C

Key Benefits: Exceptional chemical inertness and low friction

Applications: Envelope gaskets, agitator seals, instrumentation

Specific Applications

Pumps & Valves

Mechanical seals, cartridge gaskets, and valve stem packing that withstand aggressive process media and high cycle rates.

Reactors & Pressure Vessels

Large-diameter gaskets, flange seals, and manway seals delivering leak-tight performance under thermal cycling.

Instrumentation & Sampling

O-rings and diaphragms designed for analytical equipment, dosing systems, and process control instrumentation.

Bulk Transfer & Loading Systems

Hose seals, couplings, and transfer gaskets compatible with diverse chemical blends and cleaning agents.
