Automotive

Rubber o-rings and gaskets for automotive powertrain and fluid systems.

Automotive Applications

The automotive industry demands sealing materials that can endure extreme thermal cycles, aggressive fluids, dynamic motion, and long service life. From internal combustion powertrains to electrified platforms, our rubber compounds are engineered to maintain performance under elevated temperatures, high pressures, and continuous vibration.

Garlast partners with OEMs and Tier suppliers worldwide to deliver production-ready seals, gaskets, and custom components that reduce emissions, enhance efficiency, and support lighter, more compact system architectures.

Key Requirements:

  • Fuel, oil, coolant, and transmission fluid resistance
  • Wide temperature capability (-40°C to +180°C)
  • Low compression set and long-term sealing force
  • Compatibility with biofuels, EV coolants, and e-fluids
  • Low permeability to meet emissions regulations
  • Noise, vibration, and harshness (NVH) mitigation

Recommended Materials

NBR (Nitrile)

Temperature Range: -40°C to +120°C

Key Benefits: Excellent fuel and oil resistance, cost-effective

Applications: Fuel system seals, engine gaskets, transmission components

HNBR

Temperature Range: -40°C to +150°C

Key Benefits: Superior resistance to heat, ozone, and modern fuels

Applications: Turbocharger systems, high-pressure fuel rails, under-hood seals

FKM (Fluoroelastomer)

Temperature Range: -20°C to +200°C

Key Benefits: Outstanding chemical resistance and low compression set

Applications: Engine seals, EV battery thermal management systems

EPDM & Silicone

Temperature Range: -50°C to +180°C

Key Benefits: Excellent weathering, steam, and coolant resistance

Applications: Cooling systems, HVAC components, exterior weatherseals

Specific Applications

Powertrain & Driveline

Crankshaft seals, valve cover gaskets, transmission seals, and differential gaskets engineered for long-term reliability under high loads.

Fuel & Emissions Systems

Low-permeation seals, injector O-rings, vapor management gaskets, and sealing systems compatible with ethanol and alternative fuels.

Electric Vehicle Platforms

Battery pack seals, coolant lines, thermal interface materials, and dielectric seals designed for EV power electronics and charging systems.

Chassis & Interior Systems

Brake system seals, steering components, NVH isolation mounts, and interior weatherseals that improve comfort and longevity.
