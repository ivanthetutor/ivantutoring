About Garlast

Garlast Shanghai Sealing Technology Co. LTD specializes in manufacturing premium rubber sealants, gaskets, O-rings, and sealing materials for diverse industrial applications. Our flagship Garlast® FFKM products represent the pinnacle of sealing technology, while our comprehensive range includes NBR, EPDM, FKM, and other specialized materials.

With decades of expertise in rubber technology and advanced manufacturing capabilities, we deliver sealing solutions that meet the most demanding requirements across multiple industries, from automotive to aerospace, chemical processing to semiconductor manufacturing.
