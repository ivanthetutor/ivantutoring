About Garlast

Leading manufacturer of FFKM perfluoroelastomer o-rings, rubber gaskets, and premium sealing materials.

Our Expertise

Garlast Shanghai Sealing Technology Co. LTD has been at the forefront of engineered sealing solutions for over two decades. Our state-of-the-art facilities in Shanghai produce high-quality rubber sealants, gaskets, O-rings, and custom components that meet the most stringent industry standards. From Garlast™ FFKM perfluoroelastomers to NBR, EPDM, FKM, silicone, and specialty blends, we deliver materials tailored to mission-critical environments.

We combine deep materials science expertise with precision manufacturing to solve complex sealing challenges. Whether you require extreme chemical resistance, high-temperature stability, low outgassing, or FDA-compliant compounds, our products are trusted by leading companies in aerospace, automotive, chemical processing, semiconductor, pharmaceutical, and sustainable power industries worldwide.

Garlast was established with a vision to become a leading global provider of high-performance sealing solutions. Founded over two decades ago, we have grown from a specialized manufacturer to an internationally recognized company serving diverse industries worldwide.

Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility in Shanghai houses advanced production equipment, quality control laboratories, and research and development centers. We employ a team of skilled professionals, including materials scientists, engineers, and quality specialists dedicated to delivering excellence. Today, Garlast serves customers across aerospace, automotive, chemical processing, semiconductor, pharmaceutical, and sustainable power industries, providing reliable sealing solutions that meet the most demanding requirements.

Corporate Culture

At Garlast, our corporate culture is built on a foundation of integrity, innovation, and excellence. We believe that our success is directly tied to the dedication and expertise of our team members, who share a common commitment to delivering superior sealing solutions to our customers.

We foster an environment that encourages continuous learning, collaboration, and professional growth. Our team members are empowered to contribute their ideas and expertise, driving innovation and improvement across all aspects of our operations.

Our culture emphasizes customer-centricity, where every decision and action is guided by our commitment to meeting and exceeding customer expectations. We value long-term partnerships built on trust, reliability, and mutual success.

Quality Management

Quality is at the core of everything we do at Garlast. Our comprehensive quality management system is designed to ensure that every product we manufacture meets or exceeds the most stringent industry standards and customer requirements.

We maintain ISO 9001:2015 certification and adhere to rigorous quality control procedures throughout our manufacturing process. Our state-of-the-art quality control laboratory is equipped with advanced testing equipment for material characterization, dimensional inspection, mechanical property testing, and chemical compatibility analysis.

Every batch of material and finished product undergoes comprehensive testing and inspection. We maintain detailed traceability records, providing complete documentation from raw materials through final delivery. Our quality team works closely with production and engineering to continuously improve processes and prevent defects.

Research & Development

Our Research & Development team is at the forefront of sealing technology innovation. With a dedicated R&D facility and a team of experienced materials scientists and engineers, we continuously work to develop new materials, improve existing formulations, and create solutions for emerging industry challenges.

Our R&D capabilities include advanced material compounding, polymer synthesis, formulation optimization, and performance testing. We collaborate with customers to develop custom materials tailored to specific applications, working from initial concept through prototype validation and production scale-up.

Recent R&D initiatives have focused on developing ultra-high purity materials for semiconductor applications, improving high-temperature performance, enhancing chemical resistance, and creating more sustainable material options. We invest significantly in R&D to ensure we remain at the cutting edge of sealing technology.
