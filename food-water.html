Food, Water & Life Sciences

FDA-grade rubber o-rings and gaskets for food, water, and life sciences.

Food, Water & Life Sciences Applications

Sanitary processing equipment demands materials that are safe, cleanable, and resistant to CIP/SIP procedures. Our compounds are formulated to comply with FDA, 3-A, USP Class VI, and NSF requirements while delivering reliable sealing performance.

We support food processing, beverage production, biopharmaceutical manufacturing, and water treatment facilities with elastomers that resist flavor transfer, microbial growth, and harsh cleaning chemistries.

Key Requirements:

  • Regulatory compliance (FDA 21 CFR, USP Class VI, NSF/ANSI)
  • Resistance to steam, hot water, and aggressive cleaning chemicals
  • Low extractables and odor/taste neutrality
  • Microbiological resistance and easy-to-clean surfaces
  • Traceability and batch documentation
  • Color coding for HACCP programs

Recommended Materials

Platinum-Cured Silicone

Temperature Range: -60°C to +200°C

Key Benefits: Biocompatibility, sterilization resistance, low extractables

Applications: Biopharma tubing seals, peristaltic pump components, medical devices

EPDM

Temperature Range: -50°C to +150°C

Key Benefits: Excellent steam and hot water resistance

Applications: Dairy couplings, CIP/SIP gaskets, water treatment systems

FKM

Temperature Range: -20°C to +200°C

Key Benefits: Resistance to fats, oils, and aggressive cleaning agents

Applications: Beverage processing, flavor concentrate lines, sterilizers

PTFE & PTFE-Encapsulated Seals

Temperature Range: -200°C to +260°C

Key Benefits: Chemically inert, easy to clean

Applications: Pharmaceutical reactors, mixers, high-purity water systems

Specific Applications

Food & Beverage Processing

Tri-clamp gaskets, valve seats, and pump seals that prevent contamination and maintain flavor integrity.

Biopharmaceutical Manufacturing

Single-use seals, sanitary connectors, and aseptic processing components that withstand sterilization cycles.

Water Treatment & Filtration

NSF-compliant seals for filtration housing, UV systems, and disinfection equipment handling chlorinated or ozone-treated water.

Medical & Diagnostic Devices

Precision micro-seals, manifolds, and interface components for diagnostic instruments and life support equipment.
